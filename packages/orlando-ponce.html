Your Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Orlando

Welcome to the Sunshine State! After your arrival at Orlando International Airport, you'll be transferred to your hotel in the heart of Orlando. Spend the evening exploring the vibrant Downtown Disney or enjoying dinner at one of the city's many restaurants.

Day 2

Theme Park Adventure

Start your day with a visit to one of Orlando's world-famous theme parks. Whether you choose Walt Disney World, Universal Studios, or SeaWorld, you're guaranteed a day of excitement and entertainment. In the evening, enjoy dinner at one of the park's themed restaurants.

Day 3

Orlando Exploration

Explore more of Orlando's attractions, such as the Kennedy Space Center, Gatorland, or the Orlando Science Center. In the afternoon, take a relaxing boat tour of the city's lakes or visit the Orlando Eye for panoramic views. End your day with a sunset dinner at a rooftop restaurant.

Day 4

Travel to Ponce

After breakfast, you'll be transferred to the airport for your flight to Ponce, Puerto Rico. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and spend the evening exploring the historic Ponce Plaza or enjoying the vibrant nightlife of downtown Ponce.

Day 5

Ponce City Highlights

Start your day with a guided tour of Ponce's historic district, including the iconic Parque de Bombas, Ponce Cathedral, and the Ponce Museum of Art. Visit the Serrallés Castle and enjoy the panoramic views of the city. In the evening, enjoy dinner at one of Ponce's many restaurants.

Day 6

Beach Day & Departure

Spend your morning at one of Puerto Rico's beautiful beaches, such as Playa de Ponce or Caja de Muertos. In the afternoon, you'll be transferred to the airport for your return flight home, taking with you unforgettable memories of Florida and Puerto Rico.

About Your Destinations

Orlando - The Theme Park Capital

Orlando is a vibrant city known for its world-famous theme parks, beautiful weather, and diverse entertainment options. With over 50 million visitors annually, Orlando offers something for everyone, from thrilling rides to cultural experiences.

Beyond the theme parks, Orlando boasts beautiful lakes, nature preserves, and a thriving arts scene. The city's diverse food scene, shopping districts, and outdoor activities make it a year-round destination for travelers of all ages.

Top Attractions

  • Walt Disney World
  • Universal Studios
  • SeaWorld
  • Kennedy Space Center
  • Downtown Disney

Ponce - The Pearl of the South

Ponce is Puerto Rico's second-largest city and is known for its rich history, beautiful architecture, and vibrant culture. Founded in 1692, Ponce has preserved its colonial charm while embracing modern amenities.

The city is home to numerous museums, historic buildings, and beautiful plazas. Ponce's location on the southern coast of Puerto Rico also provides easy access to beautiful beaches and natural attractions.

Top Attractions

  • Parque de Bombas
  • Ponce Cathedral
  • Museo de Arte de Ponce
  • Serrallés Castle
  • Playa de Ponce
