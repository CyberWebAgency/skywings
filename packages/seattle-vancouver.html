Your Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Seattle

Welcome to the Emerald City! After your arrival at Seattle-Tacoma International Airport, you'll be transferred to your hotel in downtown Seattle. Spend the evening exploring the vibrant Pike Place Market or enjoying the city's renowned seafood.

Day 2

Seattle City Exploration

Start your day with a visit to the iconic Space Needle for panoramic views of the city and surrounding mountains. Continue to the Museum of Pop Culture, Chihuly Garden and Glass, and the Seattle Center. In the evening, enjoy dinner at one of Seattle's many acclaimed restaurants.

Day 3

Seattle Waterfront & Parks

Explore the Seattle waterfront, visit the Seattle Aquarium, and take a harbor cruise. In the afternoon, visit Discovery Park or the Washington Park Arboretum. End your day with a sunset view from Kerry Park.

Day 4

Travel to Vancouver

After breakfast, you'll be transferred to the airport for your flight to Vancouver. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and spend the evening exploring the historic Gastown district or enjoying the vibrant nightlife of downtown Vancouver.

Day 5

Vancouver City Highlights

Start your day with a guided tour of Stanley Park, one of North America's largest urban parks. Visit the Vancouver Aquarium, Granville Island, and the Museum of Anthropology. In the evening, enjoy dinner in one of Vancouver's diverse neighborhoods.

Day 6

Vancouver Nature & Culture

Visit the Capilano Suspension Bridge Park or Grouse Mountain for outdoor adventures. In the afternoon, explore the historic neighborhoods of Chinatown and Gastown. End your day with a sunset dinner cruise in the harbor.

Day 7

Departure

After breakfast, you'll be transferred to Vancouver International Airport for your return flight home, taking with you unforgettable memories of the Pacific Northwest.

About Your Destinations

Seattle - The Emerald City

Seattle is a vibrant coastal seaport city known for its stunning natural beauty, innovative tech scene, and rich cultural heritage. Surrounded by mountains, forests, and water, Seattle offers a perfect blend of urban sophistication and outdoor adventure.

From the historic Pike Place Market to the modern architecture of downtown, Seattle offers something for everyone. The city's commitment to sustainability, diverse food scene, and numerous outdoor activities make it a year-round destination for travelers.

Top Attractions

  • Space Needle
  • Pike Place Market
  • Chihuly Garden and Glass
  • Museum of Pop Culture
  • Kerry Park

Vancouver - Where Urban Meets Nature

Vancouver is a vibrant coastal seaport city known for its stunning natural beauty, diverse culture, and high quality of life. Surrounded by mountains and the Pacific Ocean, the city offers a perfect blend of urban sophistication and outdoor adventure.

From the historic Gastown district to the modern architecture of downtown, Vancouver offers something for everyone. The city's commitment to sustainability, diverse food scene, and numerous outdoor activities make it a year-round destination for travelers.

Top Attractions

  • Stanley Park
  • Gastown
  • Granville Island
  • Capilano Suspension Bridge
  • Grouse Mountain
