Your Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Abbotsford

Welcome to the Fraser Valley! After your arrival at Abbotsford International Airport, you'll be transferred to your hotel. Spend the evening exploring the historic downtown or enjoying dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 2

Abbotsford City Highlights

Start your day with a visit to the Abbotsford Tulip Festival or the Canadian Museum of Flight. In the afternoon, explore the historic downtown or take a stroll through Mill Lake Park. End your day with dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 3

Fraser Valley Adventure

Take a day trip to the Fraser Valley, visiting local wineries, farms, or hiking trails. In the evening, enjoy dinner at a local restaurant and catch a show at a local theater.

Day 4

Travel to Edmonton

After breakfast, you'll be transferred to the airport for your flight to Edmonton. Upon arrival, check into your hotel in downtown Edmonton and spend the evening exploring the vibrant Whyte Avenue or enjoying dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 5

Edmonton City Exploration

Start your day with a visit to the West Edmonton Mall, then explore the Art Gallery of Alberta. In the afternoon, visit the Royal Alberta Museum or take a stroll through the River Valley. End your day with dinner in Old Strathcona.

Day 6

Departure

Spend your final morning exploring the Muttart Conservatory or taking a stroll through the Legislature Grounds. In the afternoon, you'll be transferred to the airport for your return flight home, taking with you unforgettable memories of Abbotsford and Edmonton.

About Your Destinations

Abbotsford - The Heart of the Fraser Valley

Abbotsford is a vibrant city known for its agricultural heritage, outdoor recreation, and cultural attractions. With its stunning natural beauty and diverse community, Abbotsford offers visitors a perfect blend of urban amenities and rural charm.

The city's diverse areas each have their own character, from the historic downtown to the scenic countryside. Whether you're interested in agriculture, outdoor activities, or local culture, Abbotsford has it all.

Top Attractions

  • Abbotsford Tulip Festival
  • Canadian Museum of Flight
  • Mill Lake Park
  • Fraser Valley Wineries
  • Historic Downtown

Edmonton - The Festival City

Edmonton is a vibrant metropolis known for its festivals, outdoor recreation, and cultural attractions. With its stunning river valley and diverse neighborhoods, Edmonton offers something for everyone.

The city's diverse areas each have their own character, from the historic Old Strathcona to the trendy Downtown. Whether you're interested in shopping, arts, sports, or food, Edmonton has it all.

Top Attractions

  • West Edmonton Mall
  • Art Gallery of Alberta
  • Royal Alberta Museum
  • River Valley
  • Old Strathcona
