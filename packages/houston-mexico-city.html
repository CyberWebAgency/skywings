About Your Destinations

Houston - Space City

Houston is a vibrant metropolis known for its space exploration, diverse culture, and thriving arts scene. With world-class museums, theaters, and restaurants, Houston offers something for everyone.

The city's diverse neighborhoods each have their own character, from the historic Market Square to the trendy Montrose district. Whether you're interested in science, art, food, or entertainment, Houston has it all.

Top Attractions

  • Museum of Fine Arts

  • Buffalo Bayou Park

Mexico City - The Heart of Mexico

Mexico City is a vibrant metropolis known for its rich history, diverse culture, and exciting food scene. With its colonial architecture, ancient ruins, and modern amenities, Mexico City offers a unique blend of old and new.

The city's neighborhoods each have their own character, from the historic center to the trendy Roma and Condesa districts. Mexico City's museums, restaurants, and cultural events make it a year-round destination.
