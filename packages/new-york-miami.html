Your Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in New York

Welcome to the Big Apple! After your arrival at JFK International Airport, you'll be transferred to your hotel in Manhattan. Spend the evening exploring Times Square or enjoying dinner at one of the city's many restaurants.

Day 2

New York City Highlights

Start your day with a visit to the Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island. In the afternoon, explore Central Park and visit the Metropolitan Museum of Art. End your day with a Broadway show or a walk across the Brooklyn Bridge.

About Your Destinations

New York - The City That Never Sleeps

New York City is a vibrant metropolis known for its iconic skyline, diverse culture, and endless entertainment options. With world-class museums, theaters, restaurants, and shopping, NYC offers something for everyone.

The city's five boroughs each have their own unique character, from the skyscrapers of Manhattan to the artistic vibe of Brooklyn. Whether you're interested in history, art, food, or entertainment, New York has it all.

Top Attractions

  • Statue of Liberty
  • Central Park
  • Empire State Building
  • Times Square
  • Metropolitan Museum of Art

Miami - The Magic City

Miami is a vibrant city known for its beautiful beaches, diverse culture, and exciting nightlife. With its Art Deco architecture, Cuban influence, and tropical climate, Miami offers a unique blend of urban and beach experiences.

The city's neighborhoods each have their own character, from the historic Art Deco District in South Beach to the Cuban culture of Little Havana. Miami's food scene, shopping districts, and outdoor activities make it a year-round destination.

Top Attractions

  • South Beach
  • Art Deco Historic District
  • Little Havana
  • Vizcaya Museum and Gardens
  • Everglades National Park
