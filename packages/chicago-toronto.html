Your Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Chicago

Welcome to the Windy City! After your arrival at O'Hare International Airport, you'll be transferred to your hotel in downtown Chicago. Spend the evening exploring the Magnificent Mile or enjoying dinner at one of the city's famous steakhouses.

Day 2

Chicago City Highlights

Start your day with a visit to Millennium Park and the Art Institute of Chicago. In the afternoon, take an architecture river cruise or visit Willis Tower. End your day with dinner in the West Loop or River North.

Day 3

Chicago Cultural Experience

Visit the Field Museum or the Museum of Science and Industry. In the evening, enjoy a Chicago-style pizza dinner and catch a show at a local theater or comedy club.

Day 4

Travel to Toronto

After breakfast, you'll be transferred to the airport for your flight to Toronto. Upon arrival, check into your hotel in downtown Toronto and spend the evening exploring the Entertainment District or enjoying dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 5

Toronto City Exploration

Start your day with a visit to the CN Tower, then explore the Royal Ontario Museum. In the afternoon, visit the Art Gallery of Ontario or take a stroll through Kensington Market. End your day with dinner in the Distillery District.

Day 6

Departure

Spend your final morning exploring the Toronto Islands or visiting the St. Lawrence Market. In the afternoon, you'll be transferred to the airport for your return flight home, taking with you unforgettable memories of Chicago and Toronto.

About Your Destinations

Chicago - The Windy City

Chicago is a vibrant metropolis known for its architecture, food scene, and cultural attractions. With its stunning skyline and diverse neighborhoods, Chicago offers visitors a perfect blend of urban amenities and Midwestern charm.

The city's diverse areas each have their own character, from the historic Loop to the trendy West Loop. Whether you're interested in art, food, sports, or shopping, Chicago has it all.

Top Attractions

  • Millennium Park
  • Art Institute of Chicago
  • Willis Tower
  • Navy Pier
  • Magnificent Mile

Toronto - The Six

Toronto is a vibrant metropolis known for its diversity, culture, and urban attractions. With its iconic skyline and multicultural neighborhoods, Toronto offers something for everyone.

The city's diverse areas each have their own character, from the historic Distillery District to the trendy Queen West. Whether you're interested in art, food, shopping, or entertainment, Toronto has it all.

Top Attractions

  • CN Tower
  • Royal Ontario Museum
  • Art Gallery of Ontario
  • Kensington Market
  • Distillery District
