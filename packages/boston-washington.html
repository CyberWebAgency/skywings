Your Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Boston

Welcome to Beantown! After your arrival at Boston Logan International Airport, you'll be transferred to your hotel in downtown Boston. Spend the evening exploring the historic Freedom Trail or enjoying dinner in the North End.

Day 2

Boston Historical Tour

Start your day with a guided tour of the Freedom Trail, visiting historic sites like the Old State House and Paul Revere's House. In the afternoon, explore the Museum of Fine Arts or take a stroll through Boston Common. End your day with dinner in the Seaport District.

Day 3

Boston Cultural Experience

Visit Harvard University or take a day trip to Salem. In the evening, enjoy a seafood dinner at a local restaurant and catch a show at a historic theater.

Day 4

Travel to Washington

After breakfast, you'll be transferred to the airport for your flight to Washington. Upon arrival, check into your hotel in downtown Washington and spend the evening exploring the National Mall or enjoying dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 5

Washington DC Exploration

Start your day with a visit to the National Mall, then explore the Smithsonian Museums. In the afternoon, visit the White House or take a tour of the Capitol. End your day with dinner in Georgetown.

Day 6

Departure

Spend your final morning visiting the National Archives or taking a stroll through the Tidal Basin. In the afternoon, you'll be transferred to the airport for your return flight home, taking with you unforgettable memories of Boston and Washington.

About Your Destinations

Boston - The Cradle of Liberty

Boston is a historic city known for its rich colonial heritage, prestigious universities, and vibrant culture. With its charming neighborhoods and significant role in American history, Boston offers visitors a perfect blend of old-world charm and modern amenities.

The city's diverse areas each have their own character, from the historic North End to the trendy Seaport District. Whether you're interested in history, education, sports, or food, Boston has it all.

Top Attractions

  • Freedom Trail
  • Harvard University
  • Fenway Park
  • Museum of Fine Arts
  • Boston Common

Washington - The Nation's Capital

Washington, DC is a vibrant metropolis known for its iconic monuments, world-class museums, and political significance. With its grand architecture and diverse neighborhoods, Washington offers visitors a perfect blend of history and modernity.

The city's diverse areas each have their own character, from the historic Georgetown to the trendy U Street Corridor. Whether you're interested in politics, history, art, or food, Washington has it all.

Top Attractions

  • National Mall
  • Smithsonian Museums
  • White House
  • Georgetown
  • National Archives
