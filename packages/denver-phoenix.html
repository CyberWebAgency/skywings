Your Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Denver

Welcome to the Mile High City! After your arrival at Denver International Airport, you'll be transferred to your hotel in downtown Denver. Spend the evening exploring the vibrant LoDo district or enjoying dinner at one of the city's many restaurants.

Day 2

Denver City Highlights

Start your day with a visit to the Denver Art Museum, then explore the historic Union Station. In the afternoon, visit the Denver Botanic Gardens or take a stroll through City Park. End your day with dinner in the trendy RiNo neighborhood.

Day 3

Rocky Mountain Adventure

Take a day trip to Rocky Mountain National Park for hiking and wildlife viewing. In the evening, enjoy dinner in Boulder or return to Denver for a night out in the Capitol Hill neighborhood.

Day 4

Travel to Phoenix

After breakfast, you'll be transferred to the airport for your flight to Phoenix. Upon arrival, check into your hotel in downtown Phoenix and spend the evening exploring the vibrant Roosevelt Row Arts District or enjoying dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 5

Phoenix Desert Exploration

Start your day with a visit to the Desert Botanical Garden, then explore the Heard Museum to learn about Native American culture. In the afternoon, take a hike in the Phoenix Mountains Preserve. End your day with dinner in Old Town Scottsdale.

Day 6

Departure

Spend your final morning exploring the Phoenix Art Museum or taking a hot air balloon ride over the Sonoran Desert. In the afternoon, you'll be transferred to the airport for your return flight home, taking with you unforgettable memories of Denver and Phoenix.

About Your Destinations

Denver - The Mile High City

Denver is a vibrant metropolis known for its outdoor recreation, craft beer scene, and cultural attractions. With its proximity to the Rocky Mountains, Denver offers a perfect blend of urban amenities and natural beauty.

The city's diverse neighborhoods each have their own character, from the historic LoDo district to the trendy RiNo neighborhood. Whether you're interested in art, food, sports, or outdoor adventures, Denver has it all.

Top Attractions

  • Rocky Mountain National Park
  • Denver Art Museum
  • Red Rocks Amphitheatre
  • Denver Botanic Gardens
  • Union Station

Phoenix - The Valley of the Sun

Phoenix is a vibrant metropolis known for its desert landscapes, outdoor recreation, and cultural attractions. With its year-round sunshine and diverse neighborhoods, Phoenix offers something for everyone.

The city's diverse areas each have their own character, from the historic downtown to the trendy Scottsdale. Whether you're interested in hiking, golf, art, or food, Phoenix has it all.

Top Attractions

  • Desert Botanical Garden
  • Heard Museum
  • Phoenix Art Museum
  • Camelback Mountain
  • Old Town Scottsdale
