Rajasthan Forts and Palaces Tour

Duration: 13 Nights / 14 Days

Tour Overview

The land of kings, Rajasthan is also known for its vast array of forts, palaces, and colloquial havelis scattered across the many regions and kingdoms of the state. These powerful foundations create intrigue and fascination among tourists who wish to taste the true flavours of Indian heritage. The Rajputs were known for their architectural finesse and created some of the splendid structures that epitomise the power of their kingdom. Their powerful impacts can be discovered during the fortnight Rajasthan Fort and Palaces tour.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival – Delhi

Arrive Delhi, greeted by our representative on arrival and transferred to the hotel. (All hotels follow a 1400 hour check-in and 1200 hour check-out policy) There is perhaps no other capital city in the world as steeped in history and legend as the Indian capital of Delhi...

Day 2: Delhi

After breakfast, proceed for sightseeing of Old and New Delhi. Old Delhi: Visit the Red Fort, Jama Masjid, Chandni Chowk, Raj Ghat...

Day 3: Delhi – Mandawa

Breakfast at Hotel, Drive to Mandawa in Rajasthan (268 km-6 hrs) and check in at the hotel. Visit Castle Mandawa, the heritage hotel at the heart of the kingdom...

Day 4: Mandawa – Khimsar

Breakfast at Hotel, drive to Khimsar (275 km-5 ½ hrs) where tourists can avail the opportunity to stay at the 15th century Khimsar Fort...

Day 5: Khimsar – Jaisalmer

Breakfast at the hotel. Proceed to Jaisalmer, the heart of the Thar Desert (295 km-6 hrs) stopping for lunch en route...

Day 6: Jaisalmer

Breakfast at the hotel on the full-day Jaisalmer city tour. Sightseeing will include a visit to havelis, known for their beautifully carved fretwork, jali screens, and imposing Jain temples. Visit the Maharawal's former palace extravagantly painted in the Rajput style. In the late afternoon, relax at the oasis that led to the founding of the city, Gadi Sagar, and explore the royal cenotaphs at Bada Bagh before enjoying the desert sunset.

Day 7: Jaisalmer – Osian – Jodhpur

Breakfast at Hotel, Drive to Jodhpur. En route visit Osian, the Brahmanical temple town (260 km-6 hrs) in the Thar Desert. You can explore the real rural life by exploring ancient temples, handicraft workers, potters, and weavers. Proceed to Jodhpur and check in at hotel. Overnight in Jaisalmer.

Day 8: Jodhpur

Breakfast at Hotel, Proceed for the full day tour of Jodhpur through a visit to the magnificent Umaid Bhawan Palace, Mehrangarh Fort, from whose ramparts many legends of the region are revealed. You can also enjoy the stunning view of the winding lanes and famous blue clad houses of the city from the hill of Mehrangarh Fort, Mandore Garden, Clock Tower etc.

Day 9: Jodhpur – Kumbhalgarh – Ranakpur – Udaipur

Breakfast at Hotel, drive to Udaipur (259 km-6 hrs), visit the remote Kumbhalgarh Fort whose impregnable structure still dominates the surrounding countryside. Later proceed to Ranakpur and explore the famous Jain marble temples there. As tourists approach Udaipur, the landscape is softened by the ambiance of luxury with the tranquil setting of lakes amidst the backdrop of Aravallis. On arrival, check in at the hotel.

Day 10: Udaipur

After breakfast, visit the City Palace and enjoy the relaxing evening on the idyllic boat ride on Lake Pichola subject to water level and a visit to the island palace of Jag Mandir. There is much more to explore in Udaipur such as Bharatiya Lok Kala Mandir, Jagdish Temple, Sahelion Ki Bari, Crystal Palace etc. Overnight at Hotel.

Day 11: Udaipur – Pushkar

Breakfast at the hotel. Drive to Pushkar (250 km/5 hrs) and check in at the hotel. In the afternoon explore Pushkar Temple, Ajmer Sharif Dargah etc. Overnight stay at Pushkar.

Day 12: Pushkar – Jaipur

Breakfast at Hotel, Proceed towards Jaipur (130 km/2 hrs). In the afternoon, explore Jaipur with a visit to the sprawling City Palace, the magnificent Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds) and the 18th century observatory Jantar Mantar. In the evening, explore the colourful local markets. Overnight in Jaipur.

Day 13: Jaipur – Fatehpur Sikri – Agra

Breakfast at the hotel. Explore Amber Fort, home to a fabulous fort and palaces, where tourists ascend to the battlements via Elephant Safari. From the Maharaja's apartments, you can enjoy panoramic views of the Rajasthani countryside. Drive to Agra (232 km-5 hrs). On the way, visit the deserted city of Fatehpur Sikri, built by Emperor Akbar, abandoned four centuries ago but still perfectly preserved. Check in at the hotel.

Day 14: Agra – Delhi – Departure

Breakfast at the hotel. In the morning, visit the enchanting Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World. After the tour, drive back to Delhi for your onward journey home with cherished memories of your Rajasthan Forts and Palaces Tour.
