Greece Tour

Duration: 7 Days / 6 Nights

Tour Overview

Experience the magic of Greece, a land of ancient wonders, breathtaking islands, and vibrant culture. Our 7-day tour takes you through the highlights of this magnificent country, from the historic ruins of Athens to the stunning blue and white landscapes of Santorini. Immerse yourself in Greek mythology, savor traditional Mediterranean cuisine, and relax on some of the world's most beautiful beaches.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Athens - Arrival

Welcome to Athens! Upon arrival at Athens International Airport, meet your guide and transfer to your hotel. After check-in, enjoy some free time to relax or start exploring the vibrant neighborhoods of Athens. In the evening, join a welcome dinner at a traditional Greek taverna in the historic Plaka district, where you'll get to experience authentic Greek cuisine and hospitality.

Day 2: Athens - Historical Tour

After breakfast, embark on a comprehensive tour of Athens' historical sites. Visit the iconic Acropolis and its magnificent Parthenon, the ancient Agora, and the Temple of Olympian Zeus. Explore the Acropolis Museum, ranked among the world's top museums. After lunch, enjoy a panoramic city tour including Constitution Square, the Panathenaic Stadium, and the National Library. The evening is free for you to explore Athens' vibrant nightlife.

Day 3: Athens to Mykonos

After an early breakfast, transfer to Piraeus port to catch the high-speed ferry to Mykonos. Upon arrival, check in to your hotel and have some time to relax. In the afternoon, enjoy a guided walking tour of Mykonos Town with its iconic windmills, Little Venice, and charming white-washed buildings. The evening is free to experience the famous Mykonos nightlife or enjoy a quiet dinner by the sea.

Day 4: Mykonos - Beach Day & Delos Excursion

Morning visit to the archaeological site of Delos, one of the most important mythological, historical, and archaeological sites in Greece. Return to Mykonos for an afternoon at one of the island's famous beaches. Choose between Paradise Beach, Super Paradise, or the more relaxed Platis Gialos. Enjoy water sports, beach clubs, or simply relax under the Mediterranean sun.

Day 5: Mykonos to Santorini

After breakfast, catch the ferry to Santorini. Upon arrival, marvel at the breathtaking caldera views as you transfer to your hotel in Fira or Oia. Afternoon at leisure to begin exploring the unique volcanic island. In the evening, experience Santorini's world-famous sunset from a perfect vantage point in Oia, followed by dinner at a local restaurant with panoramic views.

Day 6: Santorini - Island Tour

Full-day tour of Santorini, including the archaeological site of Akrotiri (the "Pompeii of the Aegean"), the black sand beach of Perissa, and the traditional village of Pyrgos. Enjoy a wine tasting experience at one of Santorini's renowned wineries. In the afternoon, take a boat trip to the volcanic hot springs and Thirassia island. Farewell dinner at a cliff-side restaurant with breathtaking caldera views.

Day 7: Departure

After breakfast, transfer to Santorini Airport for your flight to Athens, connecting to your international departure. Bid farewell to Greece with unforgettable memories and experiences. End of our services.
