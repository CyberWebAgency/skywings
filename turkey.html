Turkey Tour

Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights

Tour Overview

Experience the best of Turkey in this compact yet comprehensive 5-day tour. Discover the fascinating blend of East and West in Istanbul, explore ancient ruins, marvel at natural wonders, and immerse yourself in the rich Turkish culture. From the magnificent Hagia Sophia to the stunning Blue Mosque, this tour offers a perfect introduction to Turkey's historical treasures and natural beauty.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Istanbul - Arrival

Welcome to Turkey! Upon arrival at Istanbul Airport, you will be met by our representative and transferred to your hotel. After check-in, enjoy some free time to relax or start exploring the vibrant city on your own. In the evening, join a welcome dinner at a local restaurant where you can sample traditional Turkish cuisine while enjoying views of the Bosphorus. Overnight in Istanbul.

Day 2: Istanbul - Historical Tour

After breakfast, embark on a full-day tour of Istanbul's historical highlights. Visit the magnificent Hagia Sophia, the Blue Mosque with its six minarets, and the ancient Hippodrome. Explore the opulent Topkapi Palace, once the residence of Ottoman sultans, and the Grand Bazaar with over 4,000 shops. In the evening, enjoy an optional Bosphorus dinner cruise with traditional entertainment. Overnight in Istanbul.

Day 3: Istanbul - Bosphorus Cruise & Spice Bazaar

Start your day with a visit to the aromatic Spice Bazaar, filled with exotic spices, Turkish delight, and other local delicacies. Continue with a relaxing Bosphorus cruise, offering spectacular views of Istanbul's skyline, Ottoman palaces, and the suspension bridges connecting Europe and Asia. Visit Dolmabahce Palace, the last residence of Ottoman sultans, known for its blend of European architectural styles. The evening is free for you to explore Istanbul on your own. Overnight in Istanbul.

Day 4: Istanbul - Asian Side

Explore the Asian side of Istanbul today. Cross the Bosphorus Bridge to visit Kadikoy, a vibrant neighborhood known for its markets, cafes, and restaurants. Continue to Uskudar to see the beautiful Maiden's Tower and enjoy panoramic views of the European side. Visit Camlica Hill, the highest point in Istanbul, offering breathtaking views of the entire city. Return to the European side for your farewell dinner featuring Turkish specialties and a cultural show with belly dancing and traditional folk performances. Overnight in Istanbul.

Day 5: Istanbul - Departure

After breakfast, enjoy some free time for last-minute shopping or sightseeing depending on your flight schedule. Transfer to Istanbul Airport for your departure flight, taking home wonderful memories of your Turkish adventure.
