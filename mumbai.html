Golden Triangle with Mumbai and Goa

Duration: 10 Nights / 11 Days

Tour Overview

Experience the best of India with this comprehensive tour that combines the classic Golden Triangle (Delhi, Agra, Jaipur) with the vibrant coastal cities of Mumbai and Goa. From historical monuments and royal palaces to beautiful beaches and colonial architecture, this journey offers a perfect blend of India's diverse landscapes, cultures, and attractions.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Mumbai – Arrival

Arrive at Mumbai airport, meet our representative and transfer to your hotel. After check-in, rest and relax to recover from your journey. Depending on your arrival time, you may have the opportunity to take a short evening walk along Marine Drive to experience the sunset views over the Arabian Sea. Overnight in Mumbai.

Day 2: Mumbai – Sightseeing

After breakfast, depart for a comprehensive city tour of Mumbai. Visit the iconic Gateway of India, Mani Bhawan (Gandhi's residence in Mumbai), the fascinating Dhobi Ghat open-air laundry, and take a drive along Marine Drive. Continue to the popular Juhu Beach and Chowpatty Beach, followed by a visit to the peaceful Hanging Gardens and panoramic Kamla Nehru Park. The evening is free for shopping or relaxation. Overnight in Mumbai.

Day 3: Mumbai – Jaipur

After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your flight to Jaipur. On arrival, meet our representative and transfer to your hotel. After check-in, the remainder of the day is free for you to explore the vibrant markets of the Pink City at your leisure, or relax at the hotel. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

Day 4: Jaipur – Sightseeing

After breakfast, visit the ancient Rajput capital of Amber Fort. Enjoy an elephant ride to ascend the fort (subject to availability) and explore its magnificent architecture. Inside, see the Diwan-I-Am (Hall of Public Audience), Kali Temple, Jai Mandir (Hall of Victory) with its inlaid panels, and Sukh Niwas (Hall of Pleasure). In the afternoon, visit the City Palace, the fascinating Jantar Mantar astronomical observatory, and stop for photos at the beautiful facade of Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds). Overnight in Jaipur.

Day 5: Jaipur – Agra

After an early breakfast, drive to Agra (approximately 5 hours). On arrival, check in at your hotel and freshen up. In the afternoon, visit the magnificent Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. This white marble mausoleum was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. Later, explore the impressive Agra Fort, which served as the main residence of the Mughal Emperors until 1638. Overnight in Agra.

Day 6: Agra – Delhi

After breakfast, drive to Delhi (approximately 4 hours). On arrival, check-in at the hotel and enjoy some leisure time to relax. In the evening, you can choose to explore nearby markets or enjoy the facilities at your hotel. Overnight in Delhi.

Day 7: Delhi – Sightseeing

After breakfast, depart for a full-day sightseeing tour of Old and New Delhi. In Old Delhi, visit the Red Fort (from outside), Jama Masjid (India's largest mosque), take a rickshaw ride through the bustling lanes of Chandni Chowk, and visit Raj Ghat (Mahatma Gandhi's memorial). In New Delhi, see India Gate, drive past the Parliament House, Secretariat Buildings, and President's House. Also visit the UNESCO World Heritage Site Qutub Minar and Humayun's Tomb. Overnight in Delhi.

Day 8: Delhi – Goa

After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your flight to Goa. On arrival, meet our representative and transfer to your beach resort. The rest of the afternoon is free for you to enjoy the sun, sand, and sea at one of Goa's beautiful beaches. Take a leisurely stroll along the shore, swim in the Arabian Sea, or simply relax by the pool of your resort. Overnight in Goa.

Day 9: Goa – Sightseeing

After breakfast, enjoy a full-day tour of Goa. Visit the scenic Miramar Beach, explore Panaji (Goa's capital city), wander through the charming Portuguese Latin Quarters of Fontainhas, and see the historic churches of Old Goa including the Basilica of Bom Jesus (UNESCO World Heritage Site) and Se Cathedral. Also visit the Mangueshi Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva. Return to your hotel in the evening. Overnight in Goa.

Day 10: Goa – Mumbai

After breakfast, enjoy a leisurely morning at the beach or hotel. Later, transfer to the airport for your flight to Mumbai. On arrival, meet our representative and transfer to your hotel. The evening is free for last-minute shopping or exploring more of Mumbai. Overnight in Mumbai.

Day 11: Mumbai – Departure

After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and transfer to the international airport for your departure flight. The tour ends with wonderful memories of your journey through the diverse landscapes and cultures of India.
