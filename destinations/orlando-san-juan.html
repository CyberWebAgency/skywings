About Your Destination

San Juan - The Pearl of the Caribbean

San Juan, the capital of Puerto Rico, is a vibrant city that perfectly blends Spanish colonial history with Caribbean charm. Founded in 1521, it's the second oldest European-founded city in the Americas, offering visitors a rich cultural experience.

The city is famous for its colorful colonial architecture, historic forts, beautiful beaches, and warm hospitality. From the cobblestone streets of Old San Juan to the modern amenities of Condado, there's something for every traveler.

Top Attractions

  • El Morro Fortress
  • Old San Juan Historic District
  • Condado Beach
  • La Fortaleza
  • San Juan Cathedral

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit San Juan is from mid-April to June, when the weather is pleasant and the crowds are smaller. The city enjoys warm weather year-round, but the peak tourist season is from December to April. Hurricane season runs from June to November.
