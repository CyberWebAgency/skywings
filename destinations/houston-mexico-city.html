About Your Destination

Mexico City - The Heart of Mexico

Mexico City, the capital of Mexico, is one of the world's largest and most vibrant cities. Founded by the Aztecs in 1325, it's a fascinating blend of ancient history and modern culture, offering visitors an incredible array of experiences.

The city is home to numerous UNESCO World Heritage sites, including the historic center and the floating gardens of Xochimilco. With its rich culinary scene, world-class museums, and stunning architecture, Mexico City provides an unforgettable cultural experience.

Top Attractions

  • Zócalo (Plaza de la Constitución)
  • National Museum of Anthropology
  • Chapultepec Castle
  • Frida Kahlo Museum
  • Xochimilco Canals

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Mexico City is from March to May, when the weather is mild and dry. The city's high altitude means temperatures are generally comfortable year-round, but the rainy season (June to October) can bring afternoon showers.

Travel Tips

Packing Essentials

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Light layers for variable weather
  • Rain jacket (especially in rainy season)
  • Spanish phrasebook
  • Camera for attractions

Getting Around

  • Use Metro for public transit
  • Download ride-sharing apps
  • Consider Turibus for sightseeing
  • Use official taxis
  • Walking tours available

Food & Dining

  • Try street tacos
  • Visit local markets
  • Sample Mexican cuisine
  • Try local mezcal
  • Visit food markets
