About Your Destination

Toronto - The Six

Toronto is Canada's largest city and a vibrant multicultural hub known for its diverse neighborhoods, iconic skyline, and world-class attractions. The city offers a perfect blend of urban sophistication and natural beauty, with Lake Ontario providing a stunning backdrop.

From the iconic CN Tower to the historic Distillery District, Toronto offers something for everyone. The city's diverse food scene, thriving arts community, and numerous festivals make it a year-round destination for travelers.

Top Attractions

  • CN Tower
  • Royal Ontario Museum
  • Art Gallery of Ontario
  • Distillery District
  • Toronto Islands

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Toronto is from April to October when the weather is mild and outdoor activities are in full swing. Summer months (June to August) are particularly popular but can be crowded.
