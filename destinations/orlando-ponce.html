About Your Destination

Ponce - The Pearl of the South

Ponce, Puerto Rico's second-largest city, is known for its rich history, stunning colonial architecture, and vibrant cultural scene. Founded in 1692, the city offers visitors a unique blend of historical charm and modern amenities.

The city's historic district features some of the best-preserved Spanish colonial architecture in the Caribbean, including the iconic red and black Parque de Bombas. Ponce is also home to numerous museums, art galleries, and cultural institutions.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Ponce is from December to April when the weather is dry and temperatures are pleasant. This period also coincides with several cultural festivals and events, including the Ponce Carnival in February.
