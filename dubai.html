Amazing Dubai Tour

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Tour Overview

Experience the wonders of Dubai, a city where tradition meets futuristic innovation. This 5-day tour will take you through the best of what Dubai has to offer - from the iconic Burj Khalifa to thrilling desert safaris, luxury shopping, and cultural experiences that showcase the city's rich heritage and modern marvels.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Dubai

Welcome to Dubai! Upon arrival at the airport, you will be transferred to your hotel. In the evening, proceed for the Dhow Cruise. Enjoy a traditional Arabian dinner while cruising along Dubai Creek, taking in the illuminated skyline. Overnight at the hotel in Dubai.

Day 2: Dubai City Tour & Limo Ride

Today morning, enjoy a city tour of Dubai. Visit Palm Island, take a photo at the iconic Burj Al Arab, and visit Dubai Creek, Jumeirah Mosque, Atlantis the Palm, Dubai Mall, and Jumeirah Beach. Enjoy a 45-minute ride in a stretch limo. Cap off your day with a visit to the observation deck of Burj Khalifa, the world's tallest building. Overnight at the hotel in Dubai.

Day 3: Desert Safari

In the evening, experience a thrilling desert safari on a 4-wheel drive through the golden sand dunes. Enjoy the sunset, camel rides, henna designs, and a BBQ buffet dinner at a Bedouin campsite. Experience an enchanting belly dance performance under the starlit Arabian sky. Overnight at the hotel in Dubai.

Day 4: Visit Global Village & Miracle Garden

After breakfast, visit the famous Global Village and Miracle Garden. The tour starts with Butterfly Garden, a 3D garden adorned with flowers. Global Village offers cultural experiences from over 90 countries with shopping, dining, and entertainment. Marvel at the incredible floral displays at Dubai Miracle Garden, home to over 50 million flowers arranged in stunning designs. Overnight at the hotel in Dubai.

Day 5: Departure

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to the airport for your flight back home with unforgettable memories of your Dubai adventure.
